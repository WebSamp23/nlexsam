ABOUT US

What Is N-LEX?

N-LEX

N-LEX(Nail Lovers Express Service) is a service that offers customization and personalization and nail enhancement for men and women including manicure, pedicure and nail enhancement such as acrylic, nail extension and gel nails.

The goal of N-LEX is to provide a relaxing and enjoyable experience for customers while delivering high quality nail services. In connection with the tagline it believes "nails that stun, service that's fun!".

Nail Art

Nail art, the practice of painting decorative designs on your fingernails, is a fun way to brighten up your everyday look or accessorize a special occasion outfit. There are a variety of designs available, ranging from subtle and understated to funky and outrageous.

Nail Extension

Simply put, gel nail extensions consist of hard gel polish that’s cured with a UV light over the top of your natural nail. To begin with, a nail artist or nail technician would attach a nail form underneath the tip of the nail, lengthening it. The hard gel goes on top and is sculpted to perfection. That’s the classic gel manicure technique, but it’s not the only application process.

POLICIES

Important to know

We are adopting policies at Nail Salon due to increased demand for appointments and a large increase in no-shows, cancellations without prior notice, and late clients. These policies are in place to ensure that we can accommodate all of our clients into an increasingly hectic schedule and provide our clients with services on time.

CANCELATION POLICY

When appointments are canceled without sufficient warning, businesses lose the ability to fill that appointment slot with appointments for other clients who wish to obtain service(s). Booking appointments through N-LEX (through email, social media, or phone) will be subject to the following cancellation policies for the benefit and respect of our clients and staff:

Any client who fails to cancel/reschedule an appointment without giving N-LEX at least 12 hours' notice will be considered a "Late Cancellation" and will be charged a fee equal to 50% of the booked service(s) for said appointment.

Any client who fails to show up for an appointment without notifying N-LEX will be considered a "No Show" and will be charged 100% of the planned service(s) for that appointment.

Any client who fails to show up for an appointment without first calling N-LEX will no longer be able to arrange appointments with us and will be treated as a "walk-in."

Appointments planned for the same day of service(s) may be canceled or rescheduled with at least one hour's notice without penalty. Any appointments fitting these criteria that are canceled or rescheduled with less than one hour's notice will be charged a fee equal to 25% of the planned service(s) for said appointment.

We request that all clients arrive on time, ideally 5 minutes before their appointment time. Late arrivals result in service completion delays. If you come late, every client who attends after you will receive their services late for that day. We are implementing the following timeliness norms to respect the time of our employees and other clients:

PUNCTUALITY POLICY

Any customer who arrives at least 15 minutes late for an appointment without first calling N-LEX forfeits their planned appointment and is classified as a "walk-in." "Walk-in" clients are seen by the next available nail tech on a first-come, first-served basis. If a client's tardiness, along with a lack of nail tech availability, forces them to cancel and/or reschedule their appointment, they will be considered a "late cancellation" and will be charged a fee equivalent to 50% of the cost of the booked service(s) for said appointment.

PLEASE UNDERSTAND THAT WE ARE IMPLEMENTING THESE POLICIES FOR THE BENEFIT OF OUR CLIENTS; IT IS INEVITABLE FOR CLIENTS WHO ARRIVE ON TIME TO WAIT FOR SERVICES BECAUSE A CLIENT BEFORE THEM ARRIVED LATE.

WE ACKNOWLEDGE THAT LIFE HAPPENS; IF UNFORESEEN EVENTS BEYOND YOUR CONTROL ARISE AFTER BOOKING, MAKING IT IMPRACTICABLE OR ILLEGAL TO SHOW UP FOR AN APPOINTMENT, PLEASE EMAIL US AT INFO nailloversexpressservice@mail.com ABOUT THE SITUATION AND WE WILL SPEAK WITH YOU.
